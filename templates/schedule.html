Special downloads

Scheduling the following types of links will perform special actions (like downloading the source file):

  • YouTube urls like http://youtube.com/watch?v=VIDEOID
  • Vimeo urls like http://vimeo.com/VIDEOID

Be aware just pasting the page for non-specially prepared URLs (like other non-listed video sites) will just download the web page - not the actual video!
